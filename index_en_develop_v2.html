FAQ

Do I need any experience or knowledge of Tea Ceremony?
No prior knowledge is required. We welcome beginners. Our Tea Master will guide you through the rituals, so please relax and enjoy the moment.
Do I have to sit on the floor (Seiza)?
No. All our sessions are conducted in the "Ryurei" style, using tables and chairs. There is no need to kneel, ensuring a comfortable experience for everyone.
Can I take photos?
Yes, photography is allowed. However, there may be specific moments where we ask for your attention to the ceremony. The Master will inform you of the best opportunities to capture memories.
